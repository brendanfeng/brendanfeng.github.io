Ballernoi

A visualization of a basketball defense as a Voronoi tessellation built using Javascript, D3, and the canvas HTML5 element. Real player tracking data from the 2015-2016 NBA season was imported so users can see the distance their favorite NBA players can cover. A radial gradient transitioning from red to blue emphasizes the points of strength in the defense.
